About the Brand

Riyasat is a Jaipur-based brand retailing artisanal rugs, carpets and handicrafts — offering hand-crafted, colourful products that reflect local craftsmanship.

Design Concept

A subtle shell for a vivid product

The space combines rustic and traditional aesthetics with a minimal, contemporary shell. Because the products themselves are rich in colour and pattern, the interior is kept subtle — using only restrained hints of Jaipur-inspired motifs and forms.

Rustic display systems and contemporary light fixtures create a harmonious environment that highlights the products. Materials are chosen for low maintenance while preserving elegance, warmth and visual balance.

Deliverables

From plan to visualization

Furniture Layout Electrical Layout Flooring Layout 3D Visualizations Lighting & Circulation

The design focus throughout was enhancing product visibility and the customer experience — guiding the eye to the rugs while keeping circulation calm and legible.
