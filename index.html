Introduction

I place people at the centre of every space — designing for emotion, behaviour and total sensory experience.

I'm Shruti Poojary, a third-year Environment & Space Design student from Mumbai. I believe interior design goes beyond aesthetics to shape complete sensory experiences.

With an empathetic approach, I place users' needs and emotions at the centre of every space I design. My goal is to become a conscious designer and create environments that add value to millions of people.
