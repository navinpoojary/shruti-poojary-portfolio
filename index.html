Introduction

I turn brand strategy into platform-native stories — campaigns built on audience insight and design thinking.

I'm Shruti Poojary, a brand and content marketer with an M.Sc. in Design Management from Heriot-Watt University. I plan, execute and measure social campaigns across Instagram and Facebook — most recently growing a brand by 60% in revenue and 35% in engagement.

I produce short-form video, photography, copy and graphics in Canva and Adobe InDesign — all aligned to brand tone, voice and visual identity — drawing on a foundation in spatial and interior design.
