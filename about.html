About

A brand strategist with a designer's eye.

I'm a brand and content marketer with an M.Sc. in Design Management and hands-on experience planning, executing and measuring social media campaigns across Instagram and Facebook. I grew the last brand I owned by 60% in revenue and 35% in engagement through audience segmentation, A/B-tested copy and platform-native storytelling.

I'm comfortable juggling simultaneous campaigns in fast-paced environments, building content calendars, and producing short-form video, photography, copy and graphics in Canva and Adobe InDesign — all aligned to brand tone, voice and visual identity. It's a craft built on my foundation in spatial and interior design, where I learned to put people at the centre of everything I make.
