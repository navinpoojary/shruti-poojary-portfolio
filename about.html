About

An empathetic, conscious designer.

I'm an Environment & Space Design graduate from GLS Institute of Design (2020–2024), Mumbai, with an M.Sc. in Design Management from Heriot-Watt University, Scotland. I see interior design as a force that influences contemporary culture and goes far beyond aesthetics — for me, design is about crafting total sensory experiences for the people who use a space.

I consider myself an empathetic person, and I bring that empathy into my design process. I place people at the centre of every project, aiming to understand their needs, behaviours and emotions. My long-term goal is to become a conscious designer who creates spaces that are not only beautiful, but also meaningful, inclusive and impactful.
